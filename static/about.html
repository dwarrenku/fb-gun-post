Gun violence never ends

Sutherland Springs | Las Vegas | Orlando | San Bernardino | Charleston | Fort Hood | Newtown | Aurora

Mass shootings in the United States are, unfortunately, common. They happen with such frequency, and the news flurry to follow is so loud, that it can become hard to remember the cumulative effect of gun violence. In 2016, there were 27 deaths by gun in the United States. It was so common in 2016 that the likelihood of being killed by a gun in the United States was the same as dying in a car accident in the United States.

This is a service to remind us that gun violence never ends.

Once a day, it will write a post to your facebook wall. The post will summarize the gun violence that has occured over the past 72 hours, compiled by Gun Violence Archive. That's it. The numbers are provided without commentary.

All statistics provided by the Gun Violence Archive
